Our Services

You can do everything yourself, but you don’t have to. We offer a range of services to make your life easier. Whether you need a helping hand with installation or assembly, or you want to recycle your old furniture sustainably, our professionals can do the hard work for you.

Delivery Service

Flat-packed products are easy for you to transport. But you can also arrange delivery to your home or to your business with our third-party service provider.

Assembly Service

Our products are specifically designed to be simple to assemble so that our customers can easily do it themselves. However, if you need an extra hand, we can provide a service for the assembly of your ModaBlend furniture in your home.

Planning Service

We do offer the planning service such as Kitchen planning, Wardrobe planning, Living Room storage solution and Interior design planning.
